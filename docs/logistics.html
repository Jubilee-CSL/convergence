Travel

By Air

Paris is served by two main international airports:

Paris-Charles de Gaulle (CDG) — ~35 km north-east. Take the RER B direct (approx. 40 min, €14). For ENS-PSL, exit at Luxembourg (8 min walk). For QLab, exit at Denfert-Rochereau, then Metro 4 south to Mairie de Montrouge.

Paris-Orly (ORY) — ~18 km south. Take the Orlyval shuttle to Antony, then RER B north to Denfert Rochereau (approx. 35 min total, €14).

By Rail

Paris has six mainline terminals. Travellers from London (Eurostar), Brussels or Amsterdam arrive at Gare du Nord. From there take RER B southbound (direction Saint-Rémy-lès-Chevreuse or Massy-Palaiseau) to Luxembourg — two stops, under 10 minutes.

From Gare de Lyon (TGV from southern France, Italy, Switzerland): take RER D or Metro line 14 to Châtelet–Les Halles, then RER B to Luxembourg.

Local Transport

The two venues are approximately 3 km apart; allow 15–20 minutes by public transport between them.

QLab — 1 rue Maurice Arnoux, Montrouge

  • Mairie de Montrouge ~5 min walk

ENS-PSL — 24 Rue Lhomond, Paris 5th

  • Luxembourg (RER B) — 8 min walk
  • Censier–Daubenton (Metro 7) — 5 min walk
  • Place Monge (Metro 7) — 7 min walk

Between venues: Bus line 38 from Porte d'Orleans to Auguste Comte.

A Navigo Easy card (available at any metro station, €2) or single t+ tickets cover all metro, RER (within Paris), and bus travel. A single journey costs €2.15.

Cycling via Vélib' (public bike-share) is a convenient option between venues — stations near both sites.
